Mayoi Ayase Archive

Welcome to mayoiarchive! This is a personal archive dedicated to Mayoi Ayase of Ensemble Stars!! This little corner of the internet gathers all kinds of content of him, for fellow fans to enjoy!

About This Site

This project is something I’ve had in mind for a long while. I wanted to gather everything about Mayoi into one space, both as a reference and a way to express my own love for his character. That said, I have come to realize that another Mayoi directory already exists, so please check out their site here. They made theirs first, and it's beautifully done. I want to respect and credit their work, even if I do not know them.

Credits
